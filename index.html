About Me

Emilio Gordon

Aerospace Engineer

Hello! I'm Emilio, an undergraduate student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, studying Aerospace Engineering. My research interest include Entry Descent and Landing systems, DeepSpace mission design, control systems and advanced space propulsion. I take great pride in my work as both a researcher and student for the university. Outside of academics, I'm a long-distance bicyclist with my longest tour being 970 miles! I also enjoy cooking and discovering new music. If you would like to work together or would like to discuss employment, please contact me!
